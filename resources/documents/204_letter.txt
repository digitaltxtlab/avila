1. ... If some friar should have to stay there, your paternity ought to advise him strongly not to spend much time talking to the nuns. Look, mi padre, this is very necessary. And I wouldn't even want the licentiate to do so, for even though he is so good, such goodness can give rise to bad judgments in people with malicious minds, especially in small towns, and everywhere else as well.
